About Us

        

Meet Our Team

        

          A family-owned and operated venue with a shared passion for history, hospitality, and community.         

      
    
    
      
        

Family Owned & Operated

        

          Phoenix Mill Events is owned and operated by a local family who fell in love with the history of this Ford           “Village Industry” factory and saw the potential to breathe new life into the building as a one-of-a-kind           event venue.         

        

          Our team is on-site and hands-on, from your first tour through the day of your event. We care deeply about           the guest experience and about preserving the historic character of Phoenix Mill for future generations.
