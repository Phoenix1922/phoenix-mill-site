Overview & History

      

        Built between 1920–1922, the Phoenix Mill factory produced parts for the Ford Model T and Model A and later         supported the WWII effort as part of the “Arsenal of Democracy.” Today, the restored building retains its         historic character while offering modern amenities for weddings, corporate events, and celebrations.
