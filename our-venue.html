Our Venue

        

Historic Ford Factory, Modern Event Venue

        

          Phoenix Mill offers an industrial-chic backdrop with original architecture, river views, and flexible layouts           for weddings, corporate events, and celebrations.         

      
    
    
      
        

A One-of-a-Kind Space

        

          Originally built between 1920–1922 as a Ford Motor Company “Village Industry” plant, Phoenix Mill features           high ceilings, original brick, large windows, and open floor plans that create a memorable setting for any           event.         

        

          The venue sits along the River Rouge in Hines Park, giving your guests unique views and a sense of place you           won’t find in a typical banquet hall.         

        

Flexible for Many Event Types

        

          Phoenix Mill can accommodate a variety of layouts for ceremonies, receptions, cocktail hours, conferences,           trainings, and social events. From intimate gatherings to larger celebrations, our team works with you to           arrange the space to fit your needs.
