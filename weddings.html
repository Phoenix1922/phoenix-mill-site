Weddings at Phoenix Mill

        

          Celebrate your love story in a historic Ford factory transformed into a warm, inviting event venue.         

      
    
    
      
        

A Venue with History & Heart

        

          Your wedding venue should be as meaningful and memorable as your relationship. Phoenix Mill blends historic           character with modern comfort, giving you a one-of-a-kind backdrop for your ceremony and reception.         

        

Wedding Possibilities

        

          Host your ceremony and reception on-site, or use Phoenix Mill for one part of your celebration. Our flexible           floor plan can be arranged for your vision, whether that’s an intimate gathering or a larger celebration with           dinner and dancing.
